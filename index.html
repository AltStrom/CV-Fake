Life Experience

a short summary of my life

Early Life & Education

Born in Probolinggo, East Java, in 1990 to a family of educators and small-scale farmers. Grew up helping manage his family's rice and corn fields while showing a keen interest in computers. Earned a Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems from Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) Surabaya in 2012. Completed a Master’s in Community Development & Digital Transformation from Gadjah Mada University in 2016.

Career Highlights

2013–2016: IT Trainer – Desa Pintar Initiative, East Java Trained over 800 rural youth in basic computer literacy and online entrepreneurship. Led the digitization of local village archives and public service forms. 2017–2020: Program Manager – Yayasan Tumbuh Bersama, Probolinggo Designed and launched "Kebun Digital," a smart agriculture project using sensors and mobile apps for irrigation tracking. Increased crop yield by 25% across 10 pilot villages using technology-enabled farming practices. 2021–Present: Senior Consultant – Kemkominfo (Ministry of Communication and Information Technology) Advises on national policy related to internet access expansion in outer island provinces. Helped develop the "1000 Startup Digital" roadmap for East Java, focusing on inclusive innovation hubs.

Community & Volunteer Work

Founder of “Tech Sahabat Petani”, a local group providing free digital tools and training to farmers. Organizes the annual Probolinggo Tech Camp, where youth learn coding, design thinking, and product development.

Languages

Bahasa Indonesia (native), Javanese (native), English (fluent)
